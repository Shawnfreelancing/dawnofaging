Grief Inspired Coaching in Virginia Beach

Catherine McNulty is a local Virginia Beach Grief Coach and founder of Grief Inspired, LLC.

She helps people all over the world with her powerful message: “Move Forward and Be Inspired by Grief.” Grief is a natural response to death and loss, and I'm honored to be working alongside Catherine here in the Hampton Roads area. Below is a heartfelt message from her about her work and the support she offers.

“We Grieve Deeply Because We Love Deeply”

After your loved one has died and the funeral is over, the grief process has only just begun. Grieving the loss of someone you love can affect you emotionally, mentally, physically, and spiritually—and is often more intense and complicated than you expect.

A little over 13 years ago, I lost my son, Jackson Robert. He was born prematurely and lived for only 42 days. While those 42 days were a blessing, saying goodbye to him was one of the hardest things I’ve ever done. Living without him—and living with my grief—was even harder. That’s why I’m so passionate about grief support.

Meet Catherine McNulty

My name is Catherine McNulty, and for the past 6 years, I’ve served as a grief coach and educator. I’ve supported thousands of people through my YouTube videos, grief support groups, and one-on-one coaching. I’ve worked with those who’ve lost children, spouses, parents, siblings, and close friends.

In my experience, there are three key things that help us move forward through grief:

  1. Being with others who understand.
  2. Talking about and sharing stories of the people we’ve lost.
  3. Learning about grief and how we can best support ourselves.

Grief is a normal, human response to loss—and you deserve support on that journey.
