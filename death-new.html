Death Doula Services

Don't Wait Until It's Too Late! ▶

Using a Death Doula doesn't have to be just for the last moments of one's life. We bring empowerment and peace of mind — encouraging you to take control of your life and have a voice in preparing for the end-of-life journey long before a terminal diagnosis.

Comprehensive End-of-Life Planning ▶
  • Advance directives
  • Disposition plans
  • Memorial service preferences
  • Referrals on legal topics (wills, POA, healthcare directives)
Emotional and Spiritual Support ▶

We offer a safe and compassionate space for exploring emotions, fears, and spiritual concerns. Our goal is to ensure your journey is peaceful and meaningful in a non-judgmental setting.

Legacy and Aftercare Support ▶

Legacy planning may include memory books, letters, or digital archives. After death, we support families with aftercare, grief resources, and guidance during mourning.

How Can a Doula Help You? ▶

End-of-life doula care is supportive, non-medical care that begins well before the final stage of life. The goal is enhancing presence, peace of mind, and quality of life for both you and your family — while always respecting your autonomy.

Advocacy and Holistic-Based Care ▶

We advocate for your wishes and bridge communication with family and medical teams. Our holistic care addresses emotional, psychological, and spiritual well-being.

Personalized Support & Time ▶

Unlike insurance-limited providers, we offer presence and relationship over time. From errands to emotional transitions, we’re here as your consistent, compassionate anchor.

Planning, Rates & Exclusions ▶
  • Warm Welcome Clarity Call: $70 (1 hour)
  • Overnight and on-call support available
  • Custom packages tailored to need

Not included: Medical hospice care, legal/estate services, housekeeping, meal prep.

Educational Outreach ▶

We work with aging and end-of-life service organizations to offer education on legacy planning, advance directives, and holistic care options.
