Comprehensive End-of-Life Planning

Our Death Doula works with you to create a personalized end-of-life plan reflecting your values, wishes, and beliefs. This includes:

  • Advance directives
  • Disposition plans
  • Memorial service preferences
  • Referrals on legal topics (wills, POA, healthcare directives)

Emotional and Spiritual Support

We offer a safe and compassionate space for exploring emotions, fears, and spiritual concerns surrounding death. Our goal is to ensure your journey is peaceful and meaningful in a non-judgmental setting.

Legacy and Aftercare Support

Legacy planning may include memory books, letters, or digital archives. After death, we support families with aftercare, grief resources, and guidance during mourning.

Education and Community Outreach

We connect with local organizations in Hampton Roads to raise awareness about holistic end-of-life care and available providers.

Note: This is a Private Pay NON-Medical Service. Insurance is not accepted. Because sensitive medical information may be discussed, we are HIPAA Compliant and care deeply about your privacy.
