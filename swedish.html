“Before death takes away what you are given, give away what there is to give”
– Rumi

Swedish Death Cleaning is a critical component of living purposefully, letting go of clutter, and evaluating the significance of the items in our homes. It’s about improving quality of life by learning to release things that no longer serve us.

We help people at any age shift toward a simpler lifestyle, surrounded only by meaningful possessions, and to become more comfortable with “letting go.” Reflecting on mortality can inspire gratitude, build stronger relationships, and create lasting memories.

What This Is Not

This is not a cleaning service or moving company. This is coaching and hands-on support, working interactively with you to thoughtfully declutter and take ownership of your legacy.

Who It’s For

  • Anyone overwhelmed after a loved one’s passing
  • Those preparing to downsize from a family home
  • People seeking to simplify and declutter with purpose
  • Individuals wanting to delegate possessions meaningfully

Service Benefits

  • Emotional, mental, and physical well-being
  • Practical tips for goal-setting and new habits
  • Support with legacy projects like letters, memory books, or gifts
  • Preventing post-death chaos for loved ones
